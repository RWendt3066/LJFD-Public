Overview


The Lake Johanna Fire Department was established in 1943 operating one fire station. Today, the Department operates four fire stations, and protects the cities of Arden Hills, North Oaks, and Shoreview. The Department covers 36 square miles and protects a population of about 41,000 residents.


Currently the Department staff consists of:

  • 4 Full-time staff
    • Fire Chief
    • Assistant Chief
    • Fire Marshal/Deputy Chief
    • Office Manager
  • 75 Paid on-call Firefighters

The Lake Johanna Fire Department responded to 2,875 calls in 2015. In addition to responding to fire calls, the department also responds to: Vehicle Accidents with Injuries, EMS, Ice Rescue, Water Rescue, Confined Space Rescue, and Hazardous Materials.
