Benefits


Training - The Fire Department offers free training to all firefighters. Probationary firefighters will be trained and certified in the following:


  • Firefighter I
  • Firefighter II
  • Hazmat Operational Level
  • EMT - Basic

Pay - Paid-on-call firefighters are paid an hourly rate per call and per drill. They are also paid an hourly wage for working shifts at Station 2 and Station 4 during staffed hours.


Pension - All firefighters who serve 10 years or more are eligible to collect a pension once they reach the age of 50. A minimum of 20 years of service is required to recieve the full benefits payout.
