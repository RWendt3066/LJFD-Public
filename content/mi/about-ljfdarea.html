Service Area


Our Department protects an area over 30 square miles with a combined population of about 41,000 residents. Services are provided by 5 full time employees and 65 paid on-call Firefighters, operating out of 3 fire stations, responding to calls for Fire, Vehicle Accidents with Injuries, EMS, Ice Rescue, Water Rescue, Confined Space Rescue, and Hazardous Materials.


The Insurance Services Office (ISO), which evaluates and rates fire protection services, has rated the Cities of Arden Hills and Shoreview a Class 3 ISO rating, and North Oaks a Class 4 ISO rating. (As a comparison, the City of Saint Paul is ISO Class 3)
