Eligibility


The Lake Johanna Fire Department has a solid reputation for providing high quality services to the community.


To join our team you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Have a valid MN Drivers license
  • High School Diploma or GED

You must live withnin 15 minutes of a LJFD Station:

  • Station 1: 3246 New Brighton Rd., Arden Hills
  • Station 2: 4676 Hodgson Rd., North Oaks
  • Station 3: 5545 Lexington Ave. N., Shoreview
  • Station 4: 3615 Victoria St., Shoreview
